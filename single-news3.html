    2nd place winners with Team "Avocado Toast"

    The first exhibition of physical non-fungible tokens (NFTs) in the Baltic countries, "Future," as part of the "NFThon Kaunas" hackathon.     "During the "NFThon Kaunas" hackathon, artists created unique digital NFT works with sustainability themes. Therefore, the exhibition "Future," crowning the hackathon, is a glimpse into the future not only through the lens of technology but also through the prism of sustainable living."
